Welcome to Flock.

Flock is full body gesture based game is which you control a bird migrating to a new environment. The game is mediative and relaxing is designed to be played by anyone.

Flock is a meditative game that invites you to guide a bird through a stunning canyon using your body's movements captured by a webcam. Tilt and flap your arms to navigate through the peaceful landscape, with ambient sounds and warm visuals enhancing the experience. Try and collect as many flocks of birds as you can to increase your score, and watch out for obstacles during your journey! Flock is an accessible and calming experience for players of all levels and ages, and can be played on any computer equipped with a webcam.
